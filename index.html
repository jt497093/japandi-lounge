Why Japandi Lounge

Most group accommodations focus on how many people you can fit inside.

Japandi Lounge focuses on how people connect once they are there.

Set in a restored farmhouse from the 1800s near Den Bosch, Japandi Lounge is designed around calm, balance and togetherness. The Japandi interior philosophy shapes every space — reducing noise, creating warmth and encouraging presence, conversation and genuine downtime.

  • Space to be together, without feeling crowded – generous communal areas balanced with quiet corners
  • Facilities that naturally bring people together – sauna, wood-fired hot tub, pizza oven and fire pit
  • A setting that slows you down – natural materials, soft lighting and a historic structure
  • Central, yet peaceful – near Den Bosch, but once here the outside world fades away

Whether you’re planning a family weekend, a friends’ getaway or a corporate off-site, Japandi Lounge offers more than accommodation — it offers a shared experience people remember.
