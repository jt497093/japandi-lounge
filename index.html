A mindful group stay in a historic farmhouse

Japandi Lounge is a characteristic group accommodation near Den Bosch, located in a charming farm building originally constructed in the 1800s. Carefully restored to preserve its historic character, the property combines authentic architecture with a serene, contemporary atmosphere inspired by Japandi design.

Facilities designed for connection and relaxation

  • Sauna and wood-fired hot tub
  • Wood-fired pizza oven and communal fire pit
  • Large communal kitchen, long dining table and generous lounge areas
  • Family-size trampoline for children and adults

Ideal for families, friends and corporate off-sites

Japandi Lounge is ideal for family weekends, friends’ getaways and multi-generational group stays. The location is also perfectly suited for corporate heidagen (company off-sites) with overnight accommodation, offering a peaceful environment for strategy, reflection and team building.

Optional experiences include pizza workshops, breathwork or yoga sessions, and taxi or private chauffeur transport to nearby cities.

Central location in the Netherlands

Located near Den Bosch, Japandi Lounge is within approximately one hour’s drive of Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague and Eindhoven. Nearby attractions include the Efteling theme park, Loonse & Drunense Duinen National Park, Utrecht and Breda.

In short
